Baby, I Have Your Back is a performance about embracing femininity and walking collaboratively in public space. The night walk invites everyone to wear yellow, choose a walking partner, and walk together through the night. The performance will take place between Café Saarein, Bar Buka, Lellebel and Lola the Green Aardvaark on 15 Feburary 2025 from 18:30 starting at Café Saarein.

About

Baby, I Have Your Back is a performance that explores the beauty, struggles, and complexities faced by women, trans women, feminine-presenting people, and queer people when nightwalking in public spaces. Through the act of walking in pairs and a shared getting ready routine, this work documents and highlights these experiences, inviting participation from locals and the queer community. After a year of collaborative night walks across the Netherlands, join us for the final walk.

Nightwalking Route

Wear something yellow and join us at Café Saarein to kick off the nightwalk. Pick up a flyer and a pen, and write on each other’s backs—one person records the path of the nightwalk, while the other writes a memory. Tie this special moment to your walking partner using a custom ribbon. Step out into the night and keep each other close.
